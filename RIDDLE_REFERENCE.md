# Cipher Trail - Campus Hunt Riddles

## Event Structure
- **15 Teams** divided into **3 Groups** (A, B, C)
- **5 Rounds** total
  - Rounds 1-3: Group-specific parallel paths
  - Round 4: Top 3 survivors compete (semi-finals)
  - Round 5: Final 2 teams battle (grand finale)

---

## Group A (Teams 1-5) - PATH 1

### Round 1: Tree 99 (Near Canteen)
- **Encryption**: Caesar Shift (+7)
- **Encrypted Clue**: `AHUUL EE`
- **Answer**: `TREE99`
- **Riddle**: "Two nines sit on my skin, side by side, A double mirror you can't divide. I stand where hunger meets its cure, Watching snack runs, loud and sure."

### Round 2: Broken Door (Near Church)
- **Encryption**: Morse Code
- **Encrypted Clue**: `-... .-. --- -.- . -. -.. --- --- .-`
- **Answer**: `BROKENDOOR`
- **Riddle**: "I stand by a place of prayer and peace, Wood and glass in quiet crease. Not locked tight, not fully torn, Just one small crack where time has worn."

### Round 3: Water Pool (Near Swan)
- **Encryption**: Caesar Shift (+13) / ROT13
- **Encrypted Clue**: `JNGRECBBY`
- **Answer**: `WATERPOOL`
- **Riddle**: "I hold the sky but never rain, Painted blue yet dry I remain. No splash, no wave, no swimmer's call, Still I'm a pool, small and round and small."

## Encryption Methods Used

1. **Caesar Cipher** - Letter shift (e.g., +7, +9, +13)
2. **Base64** - Text-to-Base64 encoding
3. **Morse Code** - Dots and dashes
4. **Binary** - 8-bit ASCII encoding
5. **Hexadecimal** - Hex values of ASCII characters
6. **Vigenere Cipher** - Polyalphabetic substitution with a keyword
